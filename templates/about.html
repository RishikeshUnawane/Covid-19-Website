Our Mission

We are a group of dedicated volunteers from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology who curate and verify the data coming from several sources. We extract the details, like a patient's symptoms to identify local and community transmission status to find out infection probability of patient. We never collect or expose any personally identifiable data regarding the patients.
